Top Features

Hospital Room Eligibility

Up to single private room permissible for hospitalization in this plan

Bonus on No Claim

Incremental cover up to Rs 3.3 Lacs added for every claim-free year up to a cumulative maximum of Rs 8.25 Lacs in 5 years; reduction by Rs 3.3 Lacs if there is a claim

Restoration of Cover

Full reinstatement of Sum Insured for a non-related illness if the original sum insured + no claim bonus exhausted once in policy year

Share Claim Payments

Full claim paid by Insurer;20% co-payment if age>60

Health Checkups

Health checkup permissible annually, in designated network

Maternity

This plan doesn't provide coverage for maternity & related claims

Pre existing conditions

There is a waiting period of 4 years for coverage on conditions declared and existing at the time of first purchase

Eye & Dental Covers

No cover for optical and dental treatments

In-patient Care

Before Hospitalization

Medical expenses that are incurred in 30 days before hospitalisation are covered

After Hospitalization

Medical expenses that are incurred in 60 days post hospitalisation are covered

Day Care Treatments

All day care treatments that take less than 24 hours of hospitalization due to technological advancement but otherwise would have needed a day are covered

Hospitalization at Home

Treatments done at home due to patient condition or unavailability of hospital bed are covered upto 55,000

Alternate Medicine

Upto Rs 20K for utilizing ayurveda, unani or other alternate medicine methods

Network Hospitals Covered

4900+ Network hospitals

About Insurer

Religare Health Insurance (RHI), the health insurance arm of Religare Enterprises Limited (REL), is a specialized Health Insurer offering health insurance services to employees of corporates, individual customers and for financial inclusion as well. Launched in July 2012, Religare Health Insurance has made significant progress within a short span of time, and is already operating out of 54 offices with an employee strength of 1200, servicing more than 2.6 million lives across 300+ locations, including over 1100 corporates.The company is promoted by Religare Enterprises Limited (a leading diversified financial services group based out of India) and Union Bank of India & Corporation Bank. Religare Health Insurance currently offers products in the retail segment for Health Insurance, Critical Illness, Personal Accident, Top-up Coverage, International Travel Insurance and Maternity along with Group Health Insurance and Group Personal Accident Insurance for corporates.The company has been awarded for ‘Best Technology Innovation’ at The Indian Insurance Awards 2013 and also received the ‘Editor’s Choice Award for Best Product Innovation’ at Finnoviti 2013; it was recently conferred the ‘Rising Star Insurer Award’ at The Indian Insurance Awards 2014.The Religare group has further interests across Healthcare, Diagnositics and Pharmacy through its three flagship companies - (1) Fortis Healthcare, which owns or manages 54 healthcare facilities in India, Dubai & Mauritius; (2) SRL Diagnostics, India’s largest diagnostics company with 306 networking laboratories, 6900 collection points and presence in Dubai, Sri Lanka & Nepal and (3) Fortis Healthworld chain of pharmacy and wellness stores.
